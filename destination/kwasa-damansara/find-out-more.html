My Daily TOD Connection - KL Sentral

My name is Taufiq, I’m an architect-urban designer based in Kuala Lumpur. The busy metropolitan life is certainly hectic, but that is the thrill of it! And living in The Sentral Residences in KL Sentral - Malaysia’s largest integrated TOD,

makes everything much more convenient, as amenities and transportation are always within arm’s reach. Being well versed with KL Sentral and its surroundings, I’m going to share my personal Top Things to do in KL Sentral.

Jump on the ERL to the airport in less than 30 minutes!

The Express Rail Link

Naturally, work takes me all over the city and I like to travel as fast and as comfortably as possible. On the other hand, if my destination is a bit further away, I could always e-hail a cab! When I need to travel overseas for business or leisure, I hop on to the reliable ERL KLIA Transit and travel 28 minutes to get to the airport – rain or shine! A return ticket costs RM100 (Pro tip: RM90 if you use their mobile app or station kiosk).

Explore the sights the city has to offer

Of course, living and working in KL city comes with its hustle and bustle, but public transportation around TODs make commuting affordable, practical, fast and safe. KL Sentral is truly a state-of-the-art TOD, as it seamlessly links 7 different modes of transport – Light Rapid Transit (LRT), Mass Rapid Transit (MRT), Monorel, KLIA Express, KTMB Komuter & ETS, buses and taxis. With its new, spacious clean stations and carriages, the MRT is certainly one of my favourite choices. Taking the MRT is a great choice when I work late nights too, as my meetings in the city often stretch past 10pm, I can still easily find my way back to KL Sentral just before midnight.

Visit Iconic landmarks within walking distance of KL Sentral

KL Sentral is one of the leading exponents of TODs in Kuala Lumpur and it possesses all the amenities such as business, retail, entertainment and residential areas. As an architect, I find the KL Sentral area fascinating, as it is also a historical place rife with history and culture that has grown to become an integral part of the city skyline. An architect/urban designer always has a keen eye for such ‘layers’ of the city. Within 10 minutes stroll, you can visit iconic landmarks such as National Museum of Malaysia, Lake Gardens that extend over 92 hectares of land, and bustling Brickfields – home to Little India .

Be Productive amongst Like-Minded Individuals

Working is more than just sitting in an office desk in a cubicle, and I find myself having the best ideas when I’m stimulated by my environment. That’s why one of my favourite parts about KL Sentral is Found8, a co-working space that’s modern and has an electrifying atmosphere that my clients, partners and I thoroughly enjoy meeting up in. Found8 is not just any co-working space, it is South East Asia’s sole partner for ‘Google for Startups’. Being part of that network gives you access to Google’s mentors, corporates and programmes.

Have your daily dose of caffeine

When work gets a bit overwhelming and I need a breather I go to NU Sentral, an integrated shopping complex in KL Sentral. It’s one of the best places in Kuala Lumpur where I look out for F&B places for casual gatherings as well as shopping for some of the biggest, most popular brands globally. Once in a while, when I’m feeling decadent, I treat myself to a shopping spree or when I want time to think, I go to one of the cozy cafes to realign my thoughts. My favourite cafes you may ask? My picks are either Quartet in NU Sentral or Koffee Bar by Kreate, CVSKL.

Enjoy a buffet lunch in Aloft KL Sentral

Of course, KL Sentral being in a central business district is home to a few global brands in world-class hotels such as St. Regis, Le Meridien, Hilton and Aloft Hotel. My absolute favourite is the lunch buffet at Nook by Aloft. There is a mouth-watering selection of Malaysian food such as Sarawak Laksa, beef rendang, and satay. In my opinion, it’s one of the best places to host a family lunch or dinner with a group of friends. I’m surely not alone - Nook ranks 4.5 out of 5 on Tripadvisor! I’ve found myself enjoying the facilities of these hotels when business events, conferences and conventions have been held here. It is in these places which give a hint of international prestige to KL Sentral that does more than cater to international travellers and businessmen- it enriches the TOD ecosystem.
