FUTURE-READY IN A TOD

Reaching New Heights with Technological Advances

TAKING MY BUSINESS TO NEW HEIGHTS AT PJ SENTRAL

As the owner of a Digital Marketing company, setting up my office at PJ Sentral recently has proved to be the best decision I’ve made for the business so far. Being stuck in traffic is one of my biggest pet peeves, and since opting for public transport over driving a car, my daily commute now takes me half the time it used to.

When I need to be out and about in the city for appointments, my frequent commute from Taman Jaya LRT Station to the intended destinations is often a convenient, quick and safe train ride away. It’s such a relief not having to deal with the hassle of searching for parking. Furthermore, the well air-conditioned trains provide a very comfortable ride despite the hot and humid weather. Even getting in and around different parts of PJ city are easy with plenty of public buses plying the routes right at my office doorstep.

THE ALGORITHM FOR SUCCESS

Turning visions into reality isn’t far-fetched at the 5G-ready PJ Sentral. Once it becomes widespread, programming augmented reality (AR) applications will be seamless. This enables us to create even more immersive experiences with its high bandwidth and low latency. The expansion of 5G is something I look forward to, as we are always looking for ways to innovate the tourism sector through technology.

Being a digital company, my team and I are especially excited for the improved ease of use, enhanced developer tools, improved networking and lower costs. As most of our systems are cloud-based, processing and managing a huge amount of data is not foreign to us. With 5G, data will be transmitted 10 times faster than 4G networks, making our operations and efficiency go up a notch.

PJ SENTRAL - A 5G-READY WORKPLACE

My ideal workplace is a ultra-smart workplace - in which the building responds to the users needs. Most of our colleagues work remotely and all our data is stored in a cloud based server. From virtual reality meetings to automated building management and mobile workforce, we’re talking about greater possibilities for remote working, even in areas which previously had issues with cellular connectivity.

My vision of growth for my company is to implement seamless augmented reality (AR) applications in the consumer (navigation, virtual tourism) level once 5G becomes widespread. Apple, Google, Facebook,and Snapchat already offer features and apps where consumers can use AR in their daily lives. My workplace located in PJ Sentral will eventually reach the potential to take off once 5G internet rolls out.

A digital workspace means physical meeting rooms are no longer needed — one just logs into a designated online space to interact with other remote team members. Workplaces can go fully wireless: from printers and hard drives to sensors, windows and doors. Talents can be recruited effectively from anywhere, meaning more flexible work options will be available for employees.

TEAMWORK 5.0

I’m a firm believer that teamwork is a tool for success. From brainstorming new ideas to discussing programming techniques, I’m glad we function like a well-oiled machine. Operating from a TOD benefits my team too as they aren’t deterred by the early morning dread of commute. Most of my staff take the train to work, and they walk through the doors feeling energized and are conscientious. They relay that the ease of travelling to work and having the office located next to a station allows them to engage in their hobbies on the phone or slot in an extra 15 minutes of shut eye.

TIME FOR MYSELF

Of course, there’s more to life than work. The key to achieving your life goals is to be passionate with whatever your do! I’m glad my travel-related connections in the airline industry gives me a chance to satisfy my wanderlust. Whenever I’m done prepping for a big pitch, I unwind by browsing travel deals and plan for my next adventure. Once a week, I take the team to SS15, just 9 stops away from Taman Jaya LRT for lunch and their favourite bubble tea after.

To stay fit, I set aside time to go to the gym twice a week. I take the LRT from Taman Jaya Station to KL Gateway–Universiti LRT station, which is one stop away. F45 KL Gateway is my favorite gym, hands down! F45 specialises in high intensity workouts that combine free weights and cardio. Each session lasts for 45 minutes and it gets my heart rate up to 170 bpm, and that’s when know I’m burning the extra calories.

For retail therapy, although I tend to shop online more via Shopee and Lazada, I do enjoy going to the movies or the karaoke with friends. Otherwise I’d be relaxing at home, streaming my favorite Netflix series.

TECH WARRIOR

Working in a TOD like PJ Sentral means more than just convenience for me. As an entrepreneur, I value efficiency, consistency and planning ahead — key elements I found in PJ Sentral that enable me to lead that optimal, high performance lifestyle I desire. If we were not having teleconference meetings which require high bandwidth, my clients are always more than happy to come to PJ Sentral for meetings, as they are able to experience the tech first hand.

As an innovator, I read voraciously and network with other tech-developers to keep myself updated in the tech world. Business partners, entrepreneurs and laymen are my go-to sources for talking about wide-ranging issues and uncovering solutions they seek for in the near future.

PJ SENTRAL, MY TOD UNIVERSE

Since I’ve moved the office to a TOD like PJ Sentral, I feel empowered and in control of my life. Everything has fallen into place nicely: from the pivotal location of my business base to the seamless connectivity, I’m ensuring I remain on top of my game and be future-ready at PJ Sentral.

“I believe in the digital future, and anything is possible if you dream big.”
