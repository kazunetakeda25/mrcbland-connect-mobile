Living in a TOD with Viktor and Nancy

My name is Nancy, I’m an interior designer and my passion is to liven up spaces and bring beauty into the surroundings. I look for inspiration for my work by scouting around for the latest trends - not just on the internet, but within my surroundings and amongst nature.

My partner, Viktor, works as a designer and researcher for a company that studies the technology of hydroponic planting and champions urban farming. Hydroponic planting is to grow plants inside water that has been enriched with nutrients and is soil-free. This setup is known as ‘urban farming’ and is perfect for urban environments where space is limited but there is high demand for fresh, pesticide-free fruits and vegetables.

KUALA LUMPUR AT OUR FINGERTIPS

Saturdays are our off-work days. In the morning, we wake up to the view of the shimmering river by the promenade boulevard at 9 Seputeh and the soft chirping of birds from our bedroom window. I’ve been teasing Viktor because as of late, he has put on some pounds. We make it a habit to go on an early bike ride down by the promenade along the Klang river, before going out for our brunch. The cool morning breeze was lovely as we cycled on the riverside park at VIVO, 9 Seputeh.

Living here gives us excellent connectivity from Seputeh to the rest of Kuala Lumpur. Driving out of 9 Seputeh using the recently completed OKR-NPE Link Bridge cuts travel time by half to areas such as Subang, Petaling Jaya, Bangsar and KL City. Main arterial roads such as NPE, Federal Highway and Old Klang Road links us to the rest of KL seamlessly. Now you can see why we have chosen to live in 9 Seputeh, and I can quote Victor “I love that I can be amidst all of nature’s elements and yet enjoy all the conveniences of living in the heart of KL ”.

ENCORE PATISSERIE - A HIDDEN GEM IN TAMAN DESA

Our favourite brunch spot during the weekends is Encore Café, located in Taman Desa. Dining here brings us back to the time spent in a German quaint village, where Viktor stayed for two years during his student years. Some of his favourite food served here is the quiche and chicken pie. My favourite is the fruit tart with blueberries, strawberries and grapes.

We are both coffee snobs, and this place has our stamp of approval! I usually order a Soy Cappuccino and Viktor prefers a Mocha. Encore has a range of coffee beans choices. We both ordered a Single Origin from Ethopia - tasting notes were roasted malt, raspberry and vanilla. We like to just sit and watch the world go by and catch up with one another’s week’s activities at the office.

LIVING IN A SANCTUARY

When we arrive home at 9 Seputeh, we realise that we live in a wonderful world of beauty and charm.

The pool is where we spend most of our time, with crystal clear water and a serene atmosphere, we would even have late night conversations by the pool. Memories of the time we spent in Bali is reminiscent in the design of the pool area, earning it to be our favourite spot in 9 Seputeh.

SHOPAHOLIC WEEKEND

I would usually have a long list of groceries that need to be purchased by the end of the week, and we usually head to Mid-Valley Megamall and The Gardens Mall, which are just ten minutes drive away.

The shopping experience in Mid Valley is like no other, it is our go-to place to spend our weekends. Personally, we go there mostly for the cinema and good food, but when it comes to last-minute shopping, Mid Valley is the first place that comes to mind due to its variety of shops.

It is also a place for us to meet with friends and families for special occasions with a wide variety of themed restaurants and bistros available.

CONVENIENCE OF TOD LIVING

Working in the creative industry is never easy when it comes to maintaining a well-rounded relationship with your other half. So, imagine the joy of finding out that we get to work near each other in Sentul! To say the least, we were ecstatic.

It has become a routine for us every morning to take the train to work together. With each other’s company, a train ride to work has never been so enjoyable. From small talks to meaningful conversations, or even just playful banter, our relationship flourished in a small train compartment, surrounded by the hectic lifestyles of urban dwellers.

On the other hand, saving time and expenses for petrol was definitely a plus point for us. Nothing beats the feeling of looking out of a train and seeing the morning vehicular traffic on your way to work, knowing that you’ve made the right choice by taking the train. All the while enjoying the cool breeze of air conditioning and the view on the way to work. What a bonus!

9 Seputeh is close to public transport links that make it a Transit-Oriented Development (TOD). With the rest of the city in the palm of our hands, we live in one of the most convenient spots in Kuala Lumpur. Seputeh KTM connects us to KL Sentral Transportation Hub and conveniently links us to KLIA and KLIA 2.

BALANCE OF LUXURY AND CONVENIENCE

In 9 Seputeh, there is always something for everyone. Both of us are able to maintain a balanced lifestyle - where we have the conveniences of shopping, activities and public transport within arms’ reach.
